Rigveda - Mandala 1, Hymn 48

Sanskrit (Devanagari)

सांप्रत्यं सूक्तं रचयामि देवाः ।

Transliteration

sāmpratyaṁ sūktaṁ racayāmi devāḥ

Translation (English)

This is a placeholder hymn for the gods composed now.

অনুবাদ (বাংলা)

এটি দেবতাদের জন্য বর্তমানকালে রচিত একটি সূক্ত।

अनुवाद (हिन्दी)

यह देवताओं के लिए अब रचित एक सूक्त है।

Explanation

This verse is a symbolic representation of a hymn offered to the deities.
